Carly Richardson

I am a software tester working in the UK education and library sector. I am also training to be a software developer. I am passionate about creating tech to further knowledge creation, and tech for good.
I am a Researcher in the History of Astronomy and my research interests lie in paper astronomical instruments of the early modern period, particularly volvelles.
I worked in a special collections library for several years and discovered a love of paper marbling, and creating reproductions of early modern engravings. This formed the basis of Cosmographia.
